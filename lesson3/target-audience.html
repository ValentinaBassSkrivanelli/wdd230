Target Audience

The target audience of the SOAtheory.com web site is described below: Who: IT Architects, Developers, Business Stakeholders and others interested in understanding the Service Oriented Architecture design paradigm. Age: 14 to 100 Technology: Modern and legacy solutions, Usually IT professionals involved in the design and implementation of Enterprise Solutions, large integrations, large enterprise infrastructures Income: Income levels will typically be between $60,000 and above annually. Motivation: Improve knowledge of mainstream technologies and practical application of SOA in the enterprise. Improve resume and employability.

Personas

Laís Martinez

Laís Martinez is a native east coaster who came to Utah to pursue college opportunities and never left. Currently, she works at the Utah System of Higher Education as the first appointed Assistant Commissioner for Equity, Diversity, and Inclusion. Laís received a Master's from the University of Utah in Education, Culture, and Society and started her career in education directly advising K12 students on the opportunities that exist within Utah's college and career landscape.

Suzanne P. Clark

Suzanne P. Clark is president and chief executive officer of the U.S. Chamber of Commerce, a director on two corporate boards, a former business owner, and an entrepreneur at heart. With a global perspective and a fierce commitment to free enterprise, Clark’s experience in the private sector deeply informs her leadership of the U.S. Chamber—the world’s largest business organization representing employers of every size and sector in Washington, D.C., across the country, and around the globe.
